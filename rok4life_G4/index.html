Our Story

Rok4Life, Non-Profit Organization

A simple saliva test, which will be sent to your home - and you have joined the Hadassah bone marrow pool. If in the future you are lucky and find yourself suitable, in a simple process that is important to read about, you will be able to save a person who is really your "genetic twin".

Our Mission
  • Raise awareness of bone marrow donation process in Israel
  • Increase number of volunteers in the pool
  • Find suitable donors for stem cell transplants for patients in need

The "Fight for Life" association was established in 1999 following the illness of the late Eli Elkoubi who fought cancer for a whole year "Fight of Heroes". Unfortunately, the disease finally overwhelmed him and Eli died on 10.2.2000. Eli's struggle exposed us to a general struggle of many people in Israel and the world, a struggle for the lives of adults and children whose lives might have been saved if a suitable bone marrow donor had been found for them. Eli, who managed to bring awareness to the disease of leukemia among the public in Israel, at levels that were not known before and who wanted to continue and lead the fight to higher levels, will not be able to contribute to her physical work and his contribution will be left on its spiritual side. Since the establishment of the "Fight for Life" association named after the late Eli Elkoubi, we have increased the bone marrow reserves in Israel by 250,000 samples and our hand is tilted.... We hope to reach a situation where the majority of patients in Israel will find a suitable donor in the early stages of the disease, thus saving a lot of suffering from the patients and the family members accompanying the patient. The Struggle for Life Association cooperates with the Hadassah Bone Marrow Reserve, and helps recruit potential bone marrow donors for the Hadassah Bone Marrow Reserve.
